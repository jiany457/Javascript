Every move of ours creates a world.


Information worlding/Worlding by Moves is a project that aims to explore the possibility of reflecting reality inside the virtual imaginary world. It also aims at discussing the relationship between us and the our digital metaphorical extensions, as we are living in this mixed reality booming era.
This project was inspired by the idea of "Liquid Architecture in Cyberspace" by Marcos Novak who explains in the book,
"Cyberspace subverts a relation; we are now within information. In order to do so we ourselves must be reduced to bits, represented in the system, and in the process become information anew. Cyberspace offers the opportunity of maximizing the benefits of separating data, information, and form, a separation made possible by digital technology."

As a person who has a long interest in both narratives and technique of world building, I am building nature elements inside the virtual space based on person interaction with technology in the real world.
